Using Vertex AI

Scalable, somewhat user-friendly, model training

An Overview

Why Vertex?

  • More managed environments have hardware constraints (Lightning, Gradient)
  • JupyterLab interface makes life easy
  • Slots into Google Ecosystem
  • Hardware flexibility

Why didn't we do this earlier?

  • High (ish) learning curve
  • Messy to use
  • To many options, where do I start?

Vertex AI Breakdown

Some of their product offerings

  • Data labelling
  • Workflows
  • Kubeflow clusters
  • JupyterLab Workbenches

What is a workbench?

  • JupyterLab interface to train models
  • Notebooks can be scheduled to run, and results can be stored to buckets
  • That's pretty much it :)

Scalability

Support

  • Plugs into the rest of the GCP environment pretty damn well
  • Hardware scales just like the rest of GCP
  • No storage/RAM/GPU allocation issues
